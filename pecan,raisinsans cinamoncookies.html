Pecan,raisins and cinnamon cookies

Prep time: 30 min

Yield: 5,1/2dozen

Ingredients











Steps

  1. cream butter in a large mixing bowl gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy, add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition and coffee, stirring well.
  2. combine flour, soda, cinnamon and all spices in a large mixing bowl stir in raisins and pecans to coat well add t creamed mixture.
  3. drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inche apart onto greased cookie sheets bake at 350 for 8 minutes, remove from cookie sheets and cool on a wire racks.
